The real-world recipe

  1. Boil first. Bring a pot of water to a full rolling boil before the egg goes anywhere near it.
  2. Fridge-cold egg, straight in. Lower it gently with a spoon. Cold-start times are what make this reproducible.
  3. Start the timer the second it goes in. 6:00 runny dipper · 7:00 jammy, fudgy center · 9:30 hard-boiled without the gray ring.
  4. Ice bath, 30 seconds minimum. Stops the carryover cooking exactly on time and makes the shell peel cleanly.
  5. Peel, slice, salt. Identical egg, every single run. The timer is the recipe.
